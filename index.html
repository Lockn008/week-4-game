Crystals Collector

Directions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes abovve the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game start, the game will change the values of each crystal. A new game will start automatically each time you win or lose.
